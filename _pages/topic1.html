Topic 1: Evaluation of Effective Properties of Geomaterials

Challenge

Geomaterials are inherently heterogeneous, with complex internal structures that influence their macro-scale behavior. Accurately linking internal structures to effective properties remains a key challenge in geotechnical research.

Contribution

Developed a novel method utilizing Gaussian random fields to reconstruct geomaterial microstructures, analyzing their behavior under thermal and seepage conditions. This work forms the basis for advanced uncertainty quantification in geotechnical parameters.
