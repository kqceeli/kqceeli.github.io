Topic 2: Uncertainty Quantification & Propagation in Geomaterials

Challenge

Geomaterials exhibit multiscale characteristics, but the propagation of uncertainties across different scales is still unclear. A comprehensive understanding of multiscale behaviors is critical for producing accurate and reliable geotechnical predictions.

Contribution

  • Investigated how microstructural heterogeneity impacts macroscopic anisotropy and developed theoretical models to quantify effective transport properties of geomaterials at multiscale.
  • Introduced a practical upscaling method to accurately assess anisotropy in thermal and hydraulic properties.
